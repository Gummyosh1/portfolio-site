Process

How does A Fish In Sea Work?

01

Input Tasks

Write down things you need to do for the day!

02

Complete Tasks and Go Fishing

By completing your tasks you unlock bait to fish with! Once you have enough bait, you can play one of the four fun fishing minigames to catch many fish!

03

Adventure Across the Sea

As you collect fish you'll meet villagers to donate to. As you complete their quests, you'll progress across the sea!
